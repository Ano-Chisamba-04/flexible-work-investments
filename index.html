About Our History

Flexible Work Investments was founded on over 25 years of hands-on plumbing experience. Our founder, Mr Lukas, a passionate and skilled plumber, has dedicated his career to mastering the craft and delivering reliable solutions to homes and businesses. His deep commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction laid the foundation for what Flexible Work Investments is today, a trusted name for plumbing excellence. From small repairs to large projects, every job reflects decades of expertise and a true passion for the trade.

Our Mission

At Flexible Work Investments, our mission is simple, to provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable plumbing services. We are committed to ensuring that every customer receives personalized solutions and peace of mind, no matter the size of the job.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading plumbing service provider in our community and beyond. We aim to build lasting relationships with our clients through trust, innovation, and consistent excellence, while inspiring the next generation of skilled plumbers through our dedication and passion for the craft.
