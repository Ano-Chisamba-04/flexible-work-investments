Our Journey

1995
Became a certified plumber and started learning the craft from the ground up.
2000
Uprooted my whole life and started over in a new country, building a reputation for quality and reliability.
2010
Founded Flexible Work Investments, bringing trusted plumbing solutions to homes and businesses.
2013
In 2013, Lukas took a significant step in empowering others in the plumbing field by training a group of aspiring plumbers from COSDEC Ondangwa, Montronics Success College, Namibia College Of Open Learing(NAMCOL) as well as VTC Katima. Through his mentorship and expertise, these individuals are now proud, qualified plumbers who continue to uphold the high standards of the trade. This initiative not only expanded the reach of his skills but also contributed to the development of the local plumbing community."
2024
Proudly serving hundreds of happy clients, with a continued commitment to excellence.
